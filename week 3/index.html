Starry Night

This painting dates back to the late 1800’s where Vincent van Gogh is staying at an asylum. Although the picture gives off a sense of sentiment and allure, it has a somber meaning for the artist as it depicts his mental illness at the time. It’s unsure of what the artist was thinking at the time while painting this timeless masterpiece, but one thing is for sure, Starry Night is still and always will be one of my favorite paintings.
